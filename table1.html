  • The Taj Mahal is an Islamic religious building, mosque and tomb built in the 17th century by king Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal.
  • Its chief architect was Ustad Ahmad Lahauri.

  • The building is in the city of Agra, Uttar Pradesh.

  • Widely thought as one of the most beautiful buildings in the world, it is one of India's biggest tourist attractions.

  • It is listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, together with the Agra Fort, 2.5 kilometers away.

  • It was listed as one of the 7 Wonders of the World in 2007.
